FACTSHEET: Improving ventilation in indoor workplaces
Ensuring heating, ventilation and air conditioning (HVAC) systems are well-maintained and operating properly is important for indoor workplaces to manage the risk of COVID-19 transmission.
This includes any risks associated with the HVAC system being restarted after a period of shutdown.
Visit the Resources tab to download the Factsheet.
Source: SafeWork Australia | 18 February 2021
